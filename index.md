I am a postdoctoral researcher at [Centre for Quantum Information and Communication (QuIC)](http://quic.ulb.ac.be) at the Université libre de Bruxelles, hosted by Ognyan Oreshkov.
I received my PhD at the University of Hong Kong, supervised by [Giulio Chiribella](https://qici.weebly.com/giulio-chiribella.html). 

[Curriculum Vitae](/assets/pdf/ZixuanLiuCV.pdf) (last updated June 2026).

My research interests include
- causality in physical theories,
- identification and characterization of quantum behaviors in dynamical causal structures,
- operational principles in quantum information processing.

For an overview of my research, visit [this page](/research).
